. Operating Model View

Allows manual input for KLO and TD. The Enhancements column in Operating Model becomes read-only, dynamically calculated from the Demand Planning data.

📊 Demand Planning View
This section helps forecast and prioritize work based on business needs.

The Demand Planning view captures detailed enhancement demand per product.

Suggested Features:

Work Type Forecast Table A table showing estimated demand for each product across work types (KLO, TD, Enhancements).

plaintext
| Product     | KLO Demand | TD Demand | Enhancements Demand | Priority |
|-------------|------------|-----------|----------------------|----------|
| Portal      | 2          | 1         | 5                    | High     |
| Mobile App  | 1          | 2         | 3                    | Medium   |
Priority Selector Dropdowns or tags to assign priority levels (High, Medium, Low).

Forecast Period Selector Option to choose timeframes (e.g., Q1, Q2, Monthly).

Notes Section Freeform text area for strategic notes or assumptions.

⚙️ Capacity Analysis View
This section compares available resources against forecasted demand.

Suggested Features:

Team Capacity Table Shows available hours or FTEs per team or role.

plaintext
| Team         | Available Hours | KLO Allocation | TD Allocation | Enhancements Allocation |
|--------------|-----------------|----------------|----------------|--------------------------|
| Dev Team     | 160             | 40             | 60             | 60                       |
| QA Team      | 120             | 30             | 30             | 60                       |
Utilization Metrics Automatically calculate % utilization per team.

Over/Under Capacity Flags Highlight where demand exceeds capacity.

Scenario Planning Optional sliders or inputs to simulate changes in team size or demand.
